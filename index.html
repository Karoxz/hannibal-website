About the Series

Hannibal is a psychological thriller television series developed by Bryan Fuller, based on characters from the Thomas Harris novels.

Main Characters

Hannibal Lecter

A brilliant psychiatrist and secret cannibalistic serial killer.

Will Graham

An FBI profiler with empathy disorder who can see into the minds of killers.

Alana Bloom

A psychology professor and consultant for the FBI, drawn into the Hannibal-Will dynamic.

Jack Crawford

Head of the Behavioral Science Unit of the FBI, who oversees Will and the investigations.
